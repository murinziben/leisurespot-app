Our Story

Built for Botswana,
by Batswana

LeisureSpot was founded with one simple belief: Botswana's incredible leisure and tourism experiences deserve a world-class platform.

Our Mission

Connecting people to Botswana's best experiences

We built LeisureSpot to solve a real problem: world-class experiences hidden from the people who want them most — locals and visitors alike. From a morning yoga class in Gaborone to a mokoro safari in the Okavango Delta, we make discovery and booking effortless.

We also champion Botswana's small business owners. Our vendor tools give independent operators — lodges, guides, chefs, wellness practitioners — the digital infrastructure to grow their businesses and reach more customers.
